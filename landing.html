On iPhone? Here's how it works

Force of Habit isn't on the Apple App Store — but you don't need it. The app installs straight from your browser in seconds and works exactly like a normal app: its own icon on your Home Screen, full-screen, and it runs offline.

Just open the link in Safari, tap the Share button, and choose Add to Home Screen. No App Store, no download, no account. Full step-by-step instructions are below.

Install

Two ways to get started

The app works on both Android and iPhone. Pick your path below — it takes less than a minute.

🍎

iPhone & iPad

via Safari
  • Open the app link in Safari (not Chrome).
  • Tap the Share button (the square with an up arrow) at the bottom.
  • Choose Add to Home Screen.
  • Tap Add — done! The app now lives on your Home Screen.
🤖

Android

Google Play — soon · or right now
  • Soon: download directly from Google Play.
  • Right now: open the app link in Chrome.
  • Tap the menu (three dots) in the top right.
  • Choose Install app / Add to Home screen.
